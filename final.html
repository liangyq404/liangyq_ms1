Final Outcome

Experience Flow
  1. The installation is hanging inside the exhibition hall
  2. The audience comes and see the gaps and curled layer on the surface of the object, and the audience is encouraged to peel it off
  3. The audience peels different kinds of materials off, with sense of touch/sounds that evokes sublet emotions
  4. The audience peels layer by layer, in the end the audience see the camera hiding inside.
Finding the language of the material

The "eye contact" between the object and the audience

During the process, the sound that the material makes when being peeled is like the language it uses to communicate and speak up for itself, echoing the purpose of the question: my emotion against your expectation.
