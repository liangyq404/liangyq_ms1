Day 7 | Desire to Control

Guiding questions
  1. What are we talking about when we talk about perfectionism?
  2. Is control one-sided or two-sided?
Inspiration

Many people are faced with the anxiety caused by perfectionism. This anxiety actually comes from the inability to make ourselves and people around us to exist in the way we want. The desire to control others is closely related to domination, self-validation, narcissistic deprivation, instrumental anger and negative selves. Failing to develop a correct attitude towards the power of control can cause long-term mental distress, thus it is critical to find the balance between the two parties involved in a controlling relationship.

Conceptualization

A very interesting phenomenon I found is that people who show strong desire to control others sometimes secretly desire to be controlled as well. So it occurred to me that maybe the only way to create balance between the two parties is to make them control each other.
