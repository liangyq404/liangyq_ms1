2020.10.27

Day 6 | Suspicion

Guiding questions
  1. What is the relationship between trust and suspicion?
  2. What are the results of being over suspicious?
Inspiration

I find it difficult to trust others sometimes, especially when I can’t fully understand someone else’s intention. I can easily end up guessing around which could be totally unreasonable. There are times when my guess hurt my friend’s feelings, and I am getting less and less courage to show my true thoughts.

Conceptualization

Suspicion comes from distrust. The more one suspects his/her surroundings, the deeper he/she hides him/herself. Suspicion is not what they use to attack others but to protect themselves. However, sometimes this action might as well hurt others inadvertently.

Prototype & Outcome

Guidebook Of Getting Along With Onion
Rule No1. Onions are not willing to show their true feelings. You have to peel several layers to see their heart. And it takes time. So be patient.
Rule No2. Onions are fragile. Please do not betray them after you have peeled them off.
Rule No3. If you end up tearing, don’t blame them, cause onions do not intend to make you cry.
