Day 1 | Regret

In my undergraduate, we mostly worked with user-centered design topics, such as designing for the elderly and the kids, how to improve the user experience for existing design like service design. So basically I focused on solving problems for more than 3 years, but I gradually realized that this kind of design is not what we need the most anymore, cause it sometimes cause laziness not only behavioral, but also ideological. It’s slightly different between providing the user with a better life and an easier life, and there are some issues that cannot be solved by a simple “solution”.
