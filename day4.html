2020.10.25

Day 4 | Over Association

Guiding questions
  1. How does your previous experience shapes you?
  2. How do you associate the future with your past?
Inspiration

The most comments I receive from friends is “you’re thinking too much”. Most of the time I’m overthinking cause I always tend to associate what’s happening to some previous experiences, and have so called “psychological shadow”.

Conceptualization

In this project I wish to unfold my associations and try to trace back to the previous experience I had that caused these associations.

Prototype & Outcome
  1. I bought myself a bunch of lilies last weekend, hoping to make my room smell good.
  2. I bought a scented candle. I lit it last night and put it on the table.
  3. I found the flower dying this morning.
  4. What killed it?
  5. Was it the candle? Is is poisonous???
  6. Am I the killer????!!!!
  7. lol, probably gonna kill myself with a candle one day...
  8. What's the meaning of my life anyway...
Reflection

Over association could lead to a heavy mental burden. After putting down the process of my associating activity inside my head, I realized that a lot of the associations are really jumpy or even completely make no sense...Like how does a dying flower associated with killing oneself?
