Ideas in Form Part 3 | Digital Representation

The physical prototype I made and tested last time was successful in terms of guiding the user to peel it off, and the user had the feeling of “wanting to find something in the core”, but lacking a solid context. Without the context it’s difficult to create the notion of the unspeakable emotion.
So for the digital approach, I’m thinking about using voice and sound to convey the story of the scenario. I expect this to be part of the final outcome, giving the audience a full sense of the concretized emotion, not only in the sense of touching and seeing, but also hearing and even smelling (maybe I can explore about this later).
I intend to make the peeling process starts from a shallow level to a deeper level. So the first two scenarios are more about our suspicion that are easier to talk about. And gradually goes to the more hidden emotions, which is suspicion towards our close friends and even family.
